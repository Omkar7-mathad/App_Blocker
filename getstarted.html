Managing 5G Technology in Studies

Problem Statement

Rahul, a 21-year-old student, faces both advantages and challenges with 5G technology in his studies. While it enhances learning with high-speed access, it also increases distractions and screen time, affecting his academic focus.

Challenges of 5G in Studies

  • Increased distractions due to high-speed internet.
  • Excessive screen time affecting focus and health.
  • Potential cybersecurity threats while studying online.

Solutions

  • Use productivity apps like Forest and Pomodoro timers to manage study sessions.
  • Enable website blockers to limit distractions from social media.
  • Practice digital well-being by setting screen time limits.
  • Educational institutions should provide digital responsibility guidelines.
  • Utilize 5G for educational content but avoid unnecessary browsing.
